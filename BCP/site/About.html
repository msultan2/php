What is business Continuity Management BCM?

A Holistic management process that identifies potential threats to an organization and the impacts to business operations that those threats, if realized, might cause, and which provides a framework for building organizational resilience with the capability for an effective response that safeguards the interest of its key stakeholder, reputation, brand and value-creating activities. Business Continuity Management includes Business Continuity Planning, Disaster Recovery Planning and Crisis Management.

Why Vodafone should have a Business Continuity Program?

  1. - Safety of our People.
  2. - Minimize loss of our revenue.
  3. - Protect our Business Reputation.
  4. - Customer Experience.

BCP Database Tool objectives:

- Facilitate taking the right decision during crisis.
- Manage functions headcount and match it with HR system.
- Manage transportation.
- Use it as call tree for information cascade during emergency.
- Prioritize people based on their functions criticality.
- Provide alternative contact details to be used during emergency.
- Use it as reporting tool to analyze all available information.
